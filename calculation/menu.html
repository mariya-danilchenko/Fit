Such a serving is much tastier and more unusual than just grated apples. Pour 200 ml of kefir into a deep bowl, add baking powder and mix. We drive in two eggs, 100 g of rice flour, 40 g of bran and cinnamon. Mix well with a fork until the dough is smooth. Apples are peeled, cut into small slices. Sprinkle with lemon juice if desired. Lightly grease a frying pan with coconut oil and heat it up. Dip the apple rings on both sides in the dough and put them in the pan. To make the pancakes without holes, add 1 tsp to the center of the pancakes. test. Fry until golden brown.
